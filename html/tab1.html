INTRODUCTION OF THE CONCEPTS

Electric Energy

It is the energy derived from electric potential energy or Kinetic energy the charged particles. This energy is generated by the movement of electrons from one to another. The joule or watt-second is the Fundamental unit of electrical energy.

Watt

A watt is a unit of power equal to one joule of work accomplished per second. It can be used to calculate the rate during wich energy is transported. The basic unit oF 1 watt is equivalent to 1 J/s.

Scatter Plot

It is a graph that show the relationship between to variables. It is an incredibly powerful type of graph, allowing viewers to inmmediately understand a relationship or trend that would be impossible to see in almost any other Form.

Renewable Energie

These are those energy, sources based on the use of the sun, wind, water or plant or animal biomass, among others. They are characterized by the fact that they do not use Fossil fuels - as is the case with conventional energies - but resources capable of unlimited renewal.
